Tofu Scramble

What is Tofu Scramble?

Tofu scramble is diced up tofu with a special sauce mixed with vegetables

Here are the ingredients to make this

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Spinach
  • Tomatoes

Directions

  1. Dice up one block of tofu
  2. Dice up 6 oz of spinach
  3. Dice up tomatoes and fry all together
